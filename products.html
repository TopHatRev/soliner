Our Travel Packages

Our Economy package would be considered by most other solar travel agencies as a business class.
Here at SoLiner we believe that you should be able to enjoy a luxurious trip to the system no matter your finances.
With our Economy package, you can enjoy stays from Earth to the red cliffs of Mars.

The Business package is a step up from Economy, it offers larger, more luxurious vessels and living quarters.
As well as your improved quarters, an on board restaurant and entertainment centre is included for your trip beyond the atmosphere.
The Business Package extends to the floating cities of Jupiter and the Diamond Hills of Ganymede.

The First Class package is the pinnacle of interplanetary travel, you will be provided with an entire yacht to yourself, excluding crew.
Restaurants, cinemas, golf courses and much much more are on offer, all included in the price.
As well as your time on the yacht, stop overs at our top of the line orbital stations are also included, for an extra relaxing experience.
The First Class package includes trips to the entire colonised system, all the way to the rings of Saturn.
